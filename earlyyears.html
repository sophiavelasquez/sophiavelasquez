THE EARLY YEARS

2001-2002

Gerard Way (the frontman) and Matt Pelissier (the drummer) were the original founders of My Chemical Romance. Guitarist Ray Toro was recruited shortly after Gerard Way decided that he wanted to start a band. The name of the band, My Chemical Romance, was suggested by Mikey Way (Gerard's brother) when he could not get the title of an Irvine Welsh book out of his head. This title was called: Esctacy: Three Tales of Chemical Romance.

Mikey Way decided to join the band after dropping out of college and hearing the band's first demo. While the band was with Eyeball Records, they met Frank Iero, the lead vocalist and guitarist for his band, Pencey Prep. After Pencey Prep split up, Frank joined the band in 2002. The rest is history.
