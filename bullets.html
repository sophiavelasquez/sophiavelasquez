I Brought You My Bullets, You Brought Me Your Love

My Chemical Romance's first recording sessions took place in drummer Matt Pelissier's basement, where "Our Lady of Sorrows" and "Cubicles" were recorded. My Chem (a popular abbreviation for My Chemical Romance) signed to Eyeball Records and released their debut album, I Brought You My Bullets, You Brought Me Your Love, in 2002.

Fun facts


  • The album I Brought You My Bullets, You Brought Me Your Love was produced by Geoff Rickley from the band Thursday.
  • Frank Iero recorded guitar on two tracks, since he joined the band near the end of their album recording process.
  • The album was recorded at Nada Recording Studio in New Windsor, New York in May 2002.

The storyline

This record follows a distinct storyline. Every song includes a lovestory between two characters, deemed the "Demonlition Lovers," who eventually get taken away from each other after the wonderful time they spent together. The reasons for their seperation differs from song to song. For example, the song "Sunsets Over Monroeville" tells the story a man who is forced to kill his lover due to a zombie biting her.
