ABOUT

The Black Parade

The Black Parade is My Chemical Romance's third studio album. The Black Parade was first released in Europe on October 20, 2006 under Reprise Records. The album was produced by Rob Cavallo, who is known for producing Green Day and the Goo Goo Dolls albums.

Fun facts


  • The band started The Black Parade World Tour on Feburary 22, 2007 in Manchester, New Hampshire.
  • The world tour had 138 performances worldwide. It was the longest headlining tour the band had played.
  • My Chemical Romance's song "Dead!" was added to Guitar Hero II's track list.

The storyline

This record is a rock opera that follows the story of a character called "The Patient", who is dying of cancer. The album details The Patient's life, death, and experiences in the afterlife.
