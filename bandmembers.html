THE BAND MEMBERS

My Chemical Romance consists of four band members: Gerard Way, Frank Iero, Mikey Way, and Ray Toro.

Gerard Way

  • Lead singer of My Chemical Romance.
  • Born in Summit, New Jersey on April 7, 1977.
  • Author of the hit comic book series, Umbrella Academy, which was later adapted into a Netflix show.

Frank Iero

  • Rhythm guitarist for My Chemical Romance.
  • Born in Bellville, New Jersey on October 31, 1981.
  • Frank Iero has had many solo projects, one of which includes: Frank Iero and the Future Violents.

Mikey Way

  • Bassist for My Chemical Romance.
  • Gerard Way's brother.
  • Born in Newyark, New Jersey on September 10, 1980.
  • Mikey Way co-wrote the comic Collapser with Shuan Simon.

Ray Toro

  • Lead guitarist and backing vocalist for My Chemical Romance.
  • Born in Kearny, New Jersey on July 15, 1977.
  • Ray Toro has a solo album titled Remember the Laughter.
