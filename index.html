MY CHEMICAL ROMANCE

A Brief Overview

My Chemical Romance is an American rock band that hails from Newark, New Jersey. They have four studio albums: I Brought You My Bullets, You Brought Me Your Love, Three Cheers for Sweet Revenge, The Black Parade, and Danger Days: The True Lives of the Fabulous Killjoys. The band was founded by Gerard Way and Matt Pelissier after the 9/11 attacks. Singer Gerard Way witnessed the World Trade Towers fall, and decided to start a band in an attempt to make a positive impact on the world. The band stayed active from 2001-2013 before reuninting for a renunion show in October of 2019.
